Taj Mahal, Agra

The Taj Mahal is one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a symbol of love. It was built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. Made of white marble, it is famous for its beautiful architecture and design.

Best time to visit: October to March
Famous for: Architecture, history, photography

Goa

Goa is India's most popular beach destination. It is known for its clean beaches, nightlife, seafood, and Portuguese culture. It is a perfect place for relaxation and fun activities.

Best time to visit: November to February
Famous for: Beaches, nightlife, water sports

Jaipur, Rajasthan

Jaipur is known as the Pink City of India. It is famous for its forts, palaces, and rich culture. Popular attractions include Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort, and City Palace.

Best time to visit: October to March
Famous for: Palaces, forts, culture

Kerala

Kerala is known as "God's Own Country". It is famous for its backwaters, greenery, hill stations, and peaceful environment. Kerala is perfect for nature lovers.

Best time to visit: September to March
Famous for: Backwaters, nature, houseboats
