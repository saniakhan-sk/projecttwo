About Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is one of the most beautiful monuments in the world. It was built in 1632 by Shah Jahan. It represents love and is made of white marble.

Location: Agra, Uttar Pradesh
Best time to visit: October to March
Entry fee: ₹50 (Indians)
