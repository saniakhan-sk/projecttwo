About Jaipur

Jaipur is the capital of Rajasthan and is known as the Pink City. It is famous for its beautiful palaces, forts, and rich culture.

Location: Rajasthan, India
Best time to visit: October to March
Famous places: Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort, City Palace
